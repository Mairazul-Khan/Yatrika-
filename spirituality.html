Kollur Mookambika Temple

The Kollur Mookambika Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Mookambika, located in Kollur, Udupi district, Karnataka. It is one of the most revered Shakti Peethas in South India and attracts thousands of devotees throughout the year.

Udupi Krishna Temple

The Udupi Sri Krishna Matha is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, located in Udupi, Karnataka. It was established by the Vaishnavite saint Madhvacharya in the 13th century and is known for its unique idol of Lord Krishna.

Namdroling Monastery

The Namdroling Monastery, also known as the Golden Temple, is a Tibetan Buddhist monastery located in Bylakuppe, Karnataka. It is the largest teaching center of the Nyingma lineage of Tibetan Buddhism in the world.

Chamundeshwari Temple

The Chamundeshwari Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Chamundeshwari, located on top of Chamundi Hills in Mysore, Karnataka. The temple is famous for its golden idol of the goddess and offers panoramic views of Mysore city.
